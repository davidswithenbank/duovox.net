How It Works

1

Choose Your Languages

Select a primary language and a target language. DuoVox supports 51 languages on paid tiers and 28 offline.

2

Start Talking

Hit the start button and speak naturally. DuoVox listens through your microphone and begins transcribing instantly.

3

See Both Languages

Each language gets its own panel. You see your words on the left and the translation on the right — in real time.

4

Keep the Conversation

DuoVox automatically detects who's speaking and routes text to the correct lane. Just talk naturally — it keeps up.

Accuracy Disclaimer

DuoVox uses AI-powered speech recognition and machine translation to assist communication. Like all transcription and translation tools, results may contain errors and should not be treated as a verbatim or certified record. Accuracy can be affected by background noise, accents, microphone quality, overlapping speech and domain-specific terminology. DuoVox is a communication aid and should not be relied upon for legal, medical, safety-critical, financial or official purposes. Always verify important information independently.
